Research

Current directions

I develop learning-based methods that reason about motion, structure, and visual quality while remaining useful under practical constraints.

01

Video Motion

Flow estimation, frame interpolation, temporal consistency, and motion-aligned representation learning.

02

Image Restoration

Super-resolution, self-similarity, reference-based learning, and efficient network quantization.

03

Human Understanding

Skeleton-based action recognition and human pose and shape estimation in constrained environments.

04

Efficient Vision

Architectures and training strategies that improve accuracy without increasing inference complexity.
